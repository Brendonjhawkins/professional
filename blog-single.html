SO LETS MAKE THE MOST; LIFE IS BEAUTIFUL

  • by:BJH
  • date:Jan 7, 2022

At the sound of the fireworks, marking the new collective year that is 2022 I told myself that what I am is what I am. This year, for myself, is being marked by the goals that I set and my ability to accomplish those goals. So, I'm focusing my efforts on this set of possible habits and achievements. One of them being this blog which will be used to highlight my thoughts on design, current lessons within my degree in IT Security, and also my thoughts on tech I'm using. I'm also interested in connecting with people this year; collaboration is an invaluable tool, and I'm hoping to invest in that effort. This year, I will experience greatness.

If you are still looking for that one person who will change your life, take a look in the mirror.
